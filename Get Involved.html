Jakaranda & Louis Botha Children’s Home Needs Lists

21 Houses at Jakaranda Children's Home with 12 children and 2 parents per house (250 children and 40 Childcare workers)
10 Houses at Louis Botha Children's Home with 12 children and 2 parents per house (100 children and 20 Childcare workers)

1. Food donations can be fresh, frozen or canned.
2. Food donations during office hour or after hours must be delivered at the house on duty (ask at gate)
3. Make sure to leave your contact details and email adress at the caregiver that receives your donation.
4. Caregivers and all other children's home personell are not allowed to receive cash,
5. Cash Donations during office hours must be made at the finacial office, after hours at House No. 1, make sure you receive a written receipt.
